About Me
Who I Am

I'm a Senior Multimedia Designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, with over 8 years of experience crafting visual identities, motion graphics, and social media campaigns for brands across Latin America and beyond.

I believe design should be felt, not just seen. My work lives at the intersection of storytelling, motion, and visual strategy — turning ideas into experiences that resonate.
